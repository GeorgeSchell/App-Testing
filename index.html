After installing, trust the developer certificate

Because this app was not downloaded from the App Store, iOS will block it from opening until you manually trust the developer. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  2. Go to General → VPN & Device Management.
  3. Under Enterprise App, tap on George Schell.
  4. Tap Trust "George Schell" and confirm.

You only need to do this once. After trusting, you can open the app normally.
